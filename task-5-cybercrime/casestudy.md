# Cybercrime Case Study: Phishing Attack

## Introduction
Cybercrime is a growing concern in the digital world. One common type is phishing, where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information.

## Incident Description 
In this case, a student received an email that appeared to be from a bank. The email asked the user to click a link and update their account details urgently.

Believing the email to be genuine, the student entered their login credentials on a fake website. As a result, their bank account was compromised, and unauthorized transactions were made.

## Impact
- Loss of money
- Exposure of personal information
- Emotional stress and panic

## Prevention Measures
- Always verify the sender's email address
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Use strong passwords and two-factor authentication
- Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages

## Conclusion
This case highlights the importance of cyber awareness. Users must remain vigilant and follow safe online practices to prevent such incidents.
